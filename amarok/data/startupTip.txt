<p>
Some short information about the user interface:
</p>
<p>
Use Drag-and-Drop to transfer files from the filebrowser to the playlist. To 
remove files, just drag them back from the playlist into the filebrowser. 
</p>
<p>
Use the FX button on the player to choose your available effects.
</p>
<p>
Click the analyzer to choose from the multiple analyzer styles.
</p>
<p>
Media can be dragged from Konqueror and dropped into the playlist.
</p>
<p>
You can use your XMMS visualizations with amaroK, right click the player and choose Visualizations...
</p>
<p>
You can adjust amaroK's volume using your mousewheel over the amaroK trayicon.
</p>
<p>
Click on the player window with the right mouse Button to display the options 
menu.
</p>
<p>
Have fun :)
</p>
